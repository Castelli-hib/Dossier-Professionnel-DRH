2. Data Protection Officer (DPO)

Our DPO ensures that all personal data processing operations are compliant with European regulations. You can contact them for any questions regarding your personal data.

mail dpo-contact@localauthority.gov

verified_user Secure Inquiry Form

3. Purpose of Processing

Personal data collected by the HR Directorate is processed for the following specific purposes:

  • Recruitment Management: Evaluation of candidate profiles, organization of interviews, and creation of candidate pools.
  • Payroll and Social Benefits: Calculation of salaries, taxes, social security contributions, and pension funds.
  • Career Tracking: Management of performance reviews, professional training, and internal mobility.
  • Security and Access Control: Monitoring access to government buildings and secure digital environments.
