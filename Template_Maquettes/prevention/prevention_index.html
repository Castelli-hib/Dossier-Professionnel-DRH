Prevention and Safety

Our commitment to a safe workplace. The Prevention Unit coordinates health and safety strategies to ensure the well-being of all local government employees.

01. Prevention Unit

Roles & Purpose

The unit acts as the strategic architect of workplace safety, advising the General Management on risk mitigation and legal compliance.

hub Organization

A centralized team of experts collaborating with decentralized safety agents in every technical department.

Organizational Chart

badge

Prevention Advisor (CP)

Strategic lead reporting to the DG. Oversees the implementation of the Safety Program.

engineering

Prevention Assistants (AP)

Field-based monitoring, equipment inspections, and immediate risk reporting.

group

CST (Social Committee)

Consultative body ensuring employee representation in safety decisions.

02. Missions

analytics

Risk Assessment

Systematic analysis of workstations to identify physical and psychosocial hazards before they cause harm.

school

Training & Awareness

Coordinating first aid certification, fire safety drills, and specialized machinery operation licenses.

health_and_safety

Accident Analysis

In-depth investigation of workplace incidents to implement corrective measures and prevent recurrence.
