CST – Comité Social Territorial

The principal consultative body representing both staff and the local government administration. Ensuring collective dialogue, health, and safety at work.

info Presentation & Missions

The Social Territorial Committee (CST) is the central forum for social dialogue within our administration. It is responsible for being consulted on matters relating to the collective organization and operation of services.

Its mission includes ensuring the health, safety, and working conditions of all employees. The committee meets quarterly to discuss structural changes, workplace policies, and professional equality.
