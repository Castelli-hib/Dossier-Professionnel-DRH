Commissions Administratives Paritaires (CAP)

Institutional human resources portal for local government commissions. The CAP provides advice on individual career issues for permanent civil servants.

info

Role & Operation

The Joint Administrative Commissions are parity bodies (composed of equal numbers of administration and personnel representatives) that are consulted on decisions regarding the individual situation of civil servants.

Career Management

  • Refusal of professional training
  • Availability and part-time requests
  • Evaluation and notation review

Discipline & Discharge

  • Disciplinary procedures
  • Dismissal for professional insufficiency
  • Non-renewal of contract
