General Eligibility Conditions

Seniority Requirement

Eligibility starts after 3 months of effective service. All permanent contracts (CDI) and fixed-term (CDD) > 6 months are covered.

Family Status

Benefits can be extended to direct dependents (children, spouse) through a complementary contribution adjustment.

Working Hours

Partial reimbursement applies for part-time staff (pro-rata temporis) if working less than 50% of legal weekly hours.

Annual Review

Contribution rates are reviewed every December. New rates apply from January 1st of the following year.
