Q3 Financial Integrity Protocol

Ref: LGA-HR-2024-082 Date: October 2024

1. Executive Summary

This protocol establishes the framework for financial transparency and ethical conduct within the Human Resources department for the third fiscal quarter. All administrative officers are required to adhere to the reporting standards outlined herein.

2. Compliance Requirements

Staff members handling budgetary allocations must provide weekly reconciliations. Discrepancies exceeding the established threshold of 2% must be flagged to the Department Head immediately for internal review.

3. Oversight and Auditing

The External Audit Committee reserves the right to conduct unannounced inspections of the financial records. Failure to comply with the documentation standards will result in immediate disciplinary action according to the Local Government Act Section 14.
